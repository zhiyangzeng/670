If you live in the heights and don't want to venture far for good hole-in-the-wall flavor thai food, go here. If you're making some exotic asian dish that calls for kaffir lime leaves and don't know where to find it, also go here.I love this place!  It's a small little market with random Asian snacks specializing in the Thai variety.  I like to pick up a gourd drink and peruse the half bare aisles while I wait for my food.  You may want to skip the pork buns they sell up front.  They were not fresh = (
